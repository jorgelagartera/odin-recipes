Caprese Salad

Description

Caprese Salad is a simple Italian salad that showcases fresh ingredients. It typically consists of sliced fresh tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves. The salad is seasoned with salt, pepper, and often drizzled with balsamic vinegar and extra virgin olive oil.

Ingredients

  1. Fresh tomatoes
  2. Fresh mozzarella cheese
  3. Fresh basil leaves
  4. Balsamic vinegar
  5. Extra virgin olive oil
  6. Salt
  7. Pepper

Steps

  1. Slice tomatoes and mozzarella.
  2. Arrange on a plate with basil leaves.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve as a refreshing salad.
