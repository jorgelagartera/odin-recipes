Chicken Stir Fry with Vegetables

Description

Chicken Stir Fry with Vegetables is a popular dish in Asian cuisine. It involves quickly cooking bite-sized pieces of chicken with assorted vegetables such as bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, and onions in a hot wok or skillet. The dish is flavored with garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and sometimes other seasonings or sauces.

Ingredients

  1. Chicken breast or thighs, sliced
  2. Bell peppers, sliced
  3. Broccoli florets
  4. Carrots, sliced
  5. Onions, sliced
  6. Garlic, minced
  7. Ginger, minced
  8. Soy sauce
  9. Vegetable oil
  10. Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Stir-fry chicken until cooked.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, then vegetables.
  3. Cook until tender-crisp.
  4. Add soy sauce and season to taste.
  5. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
