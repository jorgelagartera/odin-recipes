Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate Chip Cookies are a classic American dessert. They are soft, chewy cookies studded with chocolate chips. The dough typically includes ingredients like butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, flour, baking soda, and salt. Chocolate Chip Cookies are a favorite treat enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  1. Butter
  2. Brown sugar
  3. Granulated sugar
  4. Eggs
  5. Vanilla extract
  6. Flour
  7. Baking soda
  8. Salt
  9. Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Cream butter and sugars.
  2. Add eggs and vanilla extract.
  3. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheet.
  6. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
