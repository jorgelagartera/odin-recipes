Vegetarian Chili

Description

Vegetarian Chili is a hearty and nutritious dish made without meat. It typically includes kidney beans, black beans, diced tomatoes, bell peppers, onions, garlic, and a blend of spices such as chili powder and cumin. Vegetable broth is used as the base to simmer all the ingredients together, creating a flavorful and satisfying chili that is both comforting and wholesome.

Ingredients

  1. Kidney beans
  2. Black beans
  3. Diced tomatoes
  4. Bell peppers
  5. Onions
  6. Garlic
  7. Chili powder
  8. Cumin
  9. Vegetable broth

  3. Simmer until flavors meld.
  4. Serve hot with toppings like sour cream, cheese, and cilantro.
