Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Classic Spaghetti Carbonara is a traditional Italian pasta dish known for its creamy texture and rich flavor. It consists of spaghetti pasta tossed with a sauce made from eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, pancetta or guanciale (Italian cured pork), black pepper, and sometimes garlic. The heat from the pasta cooks the eggs, creating a silky sauce that coats the noodles.

Ingredients

  1. Spaghetti
  2. Pancetta or guanciale
  3. Eggs
  4. Parmesan cheese
  5. Black pepper
  6. Garlic (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Toss hot pasta with pancetta.
  5. Stir in egg mixture off heat.
  6. Serve immediately.
